About Me

Hi, I’m Shubham Agarwal and I’ve been a freelance technology journalist for over three years. I cover a vast gamut of topics, most of which fall under personal technology. Instead of simply dumping a deluge of information on users, I strive to write in formats which are more approachable and make sense to users who aren’t necessarily well versed with the intricate world of technology. Academically, I’m a computer science graduate with a specialization in mobile applications.
